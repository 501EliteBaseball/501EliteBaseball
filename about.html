Our Story

A founding team with a standard to protect.

501 Elite was created for families who want youth baseball to be competitive, organized, and meaningful without losing sight of the fact that these are young kids learning the game.

Our goal is to develop players who can handle instruction, respond to adversity, celebrate teammates, and represent their families the right way. Wins matter, but the habits built along the way matter more.

This program is built around a simple belief: if we demand effort, teach character, and provide high-level instruction, baseball becomes a powerful tool for growth far beyond the field.
